If you follow this interest you will start receiving notifications when the Bitcoin to USD price reaches 100K.

Rate Limiting

   The source used in the example above issues tickers approximately every 5 seconds. Hence, once all interest criteria matches you will start to receive notifications too frequently. The best solution is to receive a notification once only and not more than once per, let's say 1 hour. There's a new soft rate limit available for followers in Telegram. You choose the minimum interval and all extra notifications will be discarded. Feel free to try!
