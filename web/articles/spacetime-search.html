On Search In Space And Time

  The role of a chance in our life is great. It's always about finding of a relevant opportunity. Whether it's a dream job, car, home or a partner.

  Unfortunately, an opportunity is generally not available immediately. When we don't find exactly what is needed, there are few options:

  • search wider, include more sources
  • wait until match appears
  • weaken the search criteria.

    •   Every option has specific pros and cons. Using a search online means one can not search wider anymore. No luck? Try again later or sacrifice your requirements.

      Instead of repeating a search again and again we usually subscribe to important sources. The problem is that to maximize the chance to catch an opportunity it's necessary to subscribe to nearly everything.

      It's okay to filter content on the client side when you have limited number of well-known sources. This is how social networks, messengers, news readers, email clients use to work. Just follow/join to limited set of groups or channels. This gives you a comfortable control but with a price. You'll never catch any serendipitous message. Just because you don't know where this important message comes from. You remain in your limited bubble missing new things to know.
